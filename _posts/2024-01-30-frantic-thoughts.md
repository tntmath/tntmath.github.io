&nbsp;It feels fucking awful. I am having a deep state of analysis paralysis. I feel like whatever I touched, I destroyed it. I looked around, cannot help but feeling a sense of sadness and pity for myself. Why things have gotten so bad lately? 

&nbsp;People went through depression, people got better in just one or two days, how can they do that? I guess when they have a permanent job, the fear is somewhat lessen. The problem I keep repeating is comparing, observing and then beating myself up. They said “insanity is doing things exactly the same but expecting different results”, well, I think I am insane. 

&nbsp;I could not figured out why life has been so rough lately. Sometime calming down, thinking, in the end, does it matter? Missing a deadline, missing a project, putting them on top of health, or time for the people I love, at the end, do they matter? I mean, deep down, I probably still care about a future where the location I ended up might have a big toll on my thinking, so I guess it matters to me, in a way that I bitterly accepted. 

&nbsp;I have lost my critical thinking skills, my attention span now is short, my concentration is weak. Looking around, I don’t see the sense of belonging as well. A couple of years devoted into something I was not even sure, what was I chasing in the end? I always thought I was chasing stability, making myself as good as Robert Lee, the legendary general to make me feel better. It turns out completely funny, I was chasing something I did not even know how to define, sometime I did not chase anything at all, just lay there, wait and wait. But then that feeling got old pretty quickly and I got bored, I started to chase something again, and never being consistent. I thought I have found a way, but no, I am still lost. 

&nbsp;Enough rambling, probably lacking of sleep is something I will never be able to fix.
