# tntmath

## This Made Sense in My Head
<!-- _Keep it simple, but not simpler_ -->

This is where I try to make sense of math and science, mostly for my own understanding. It’s not about being perfect or comprehensive, just honest attempts at clarity. Explanations are simple (but not too simple), sometimes wrong (but in interesting ways), and always a little personal. Think of it as my mathematical notebook, written out loud.
